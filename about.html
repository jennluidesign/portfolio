About

My Story

My name’s Jenn, and I‘m a UI Designer. I first dipped into the world of UI design while working as a recruiter for a major art museum. Tasked with searching for a UI/UX Designer, I learned about the role, and thought, “I would love to do something like this!”

While my background is in Human Resources, I gravitated towards UI Design for a similar reason: the desire to assist others and ensure an enjoyable experience. I wanted to create websites and apps that were intuitive and easy to navigate, while looking great.

I later enrolled myself into a UI Immersion course from Career Foundry, where I learned the building blocks and basic skills to create products of my own.

I currently live in New York’s beautiful Hudson Valley, and have a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology from the University at Albany. I love baking, hiking, and a generous dose of Netflix.
