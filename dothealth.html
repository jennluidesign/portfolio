DotHealth

Overview

Dot Health is an app which helps users keep track of their health habits and activities. Users can log their day-to-day activity on the app and get an overview of their behavior over time from the convenience of their phone.

Approach

Objective

There is currently a huge market for self-care, health, and wellness. People are striving to be the best versions of themselves, but at times, this journey can be overwhelming. Dot Health allows for an easy and convenient way for users to to keep track of their progress. Users can log their daily activities and view activity history. Dot Health helps users spot activity patterns over time, and can help hold users accountable in completing healthy activities daily. Dot Health is designed as a native mobile app for both Android and iOS devices.

What's Already Out There?

I identified two apps with similar functions to my product: Daylio and Bearable. Both provided good examples of ideas to include or consider: initial preference selection; different journal entry styles; and gamification to keep the user coming back for more. Daylio and Bearable both allow for different degrees of customization to their user experience. Where Bearable allows the user to set reminders for daily log entry, Daylio also includes options for color scheme, activities to track, and activity goals. For my project, some level of customization will be necessary, given the different priorities each user will have while using the app.

User Flow

Keeping existing products in mind, it was time to determine what this app needed to be able to do. Users needed to be able to select preferences (i.e. which health trackers they would use), record a journal entry, and view activity history.
