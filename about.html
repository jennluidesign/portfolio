About

My Story

My name’s Jenn, and I‘m a UI Designer. I currently live in New York’s beautiful Hudson Valley and have a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology from the University at Albany. I first dipped into the world of UI design while working as a recruiter. Tasked with searching for a UI/UX Designer, I learned about the role and thought, “I would love to do something like this!”

While my background is in Human Resources, I gravitated towards UI Design for similar reasons: an interest in learning about people, the desire to be helpful, and the reward of creating an enjoyable experience. People-centered thinking has always been at the forefront of my work, whether it's listening and understanding needs or crafting messages to ensure that they're clear and engaging to your specific audience.

I later enrolled myself in a UI Immersion course from CareerFoundry and learned the building blocks and basic skills to create clean and effective designs. In the future, my goal is to take the customer-first approach so prevalent in my past work experience and apply it towards a more visually oriented role in UI Design. I want to create websites and apps that are intuitive and easy to navigate while looking great.

I'm currently open to full-time work in the New York metro area or remotely.
